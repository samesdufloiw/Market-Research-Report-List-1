<p><p>The conveyor equipment market encompasses various types, each serving distinct functions. Roller conveyors utilize rollers to facilitate material movement, ideal for heavy loads. Belt conveyors use a continuous belt for transporting goods, suitable for diverse applications. Overhead conveyors maximize floor space by moving items above ground. Pallet conveyors are designed for transporting palletized loads efficiently. Additionally, the "other conveyors" category includes specialized systems like screw or chain conveyors, catering to unique industrial needs and enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
