Latest trends in the market include advanced production techniques, leading to innovative designs that mimic natural materials like wood and stone. Additionally, the emphasis on green building practices is prompting manufacturers to develop low-VOC and recyclable VCT options. As consumer preferences evolve, the incorporation of enhanced features such as antimicrobial properties and improved comfort underfoot is becoming increasingly prevalent.
