<p><p>The Direct Thermal Printers market is divided into two main types: desktop and mobile. Desktop direct thermal printers are designed for stationary use, often found in offices and retail environments, providing high-volume printing with durability and efficiency. In contrast, mobile direct thermal printers are portable and battery-operated, ideal for on-the-go printing needs such as field services, ticketing, and event management.
