<p><p>The pneumatic files market is segmented into two main types: reciprocating engine and vane engine. Reciprocating engines employ a piston mechanism that generates linear motion, offering precision in applications such as woodworking and metalworking. Vane engines use rotating vanes to create airflow, providing a smoother and quieter operation, often suited for applications requiring consistent performance. Both types cater to various industrial needs, enhancing efficiency and productivity in tasks involving material shaping and finishing.</p></p>
