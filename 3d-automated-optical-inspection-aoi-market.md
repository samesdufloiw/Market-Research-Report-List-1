<p><p>3D Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) is a technology used in manufacturing to detect defects in printed circuit boards (PCBs) and other components. The market comprises two main types: Inline 3D AOI, which integrates seamlessly into production lines, enabling real-time inspection and immediate feedback for quality control, and Offline 3D AOI, where inspections occur at separate stations after production, allowing for thorough evaluation without disrupting the manufacturing process. Both types enhance product quality and reduce defect rates.</p></p>
