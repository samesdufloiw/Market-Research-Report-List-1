<p><ul><li>Low-Pressure</li><li>Atmospheric Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cold plasma technology is categorized into two main market types: low-pressure and atmospheric pressure systems. Low-pressure cold plasma operates in a vacuum environment, ideal for applications requiring uniform treatment and precision, such as semiconductor manufacturing and surface modification. In contrast, atmospheric pressure cold plasma operates at ambient conditions, making it accessible for diverse applications like sterilization, wound healing, and agricultural enhancements. Both types leverage ionized gas to facilitate chemical reactions beneficial in various industrial and medical fields.</p></p>
