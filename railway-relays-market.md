<p><strong>The Railway Relays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Signalling Relays</li><li>Rolling Stock Relays</li><li>Auxiliary Relays for Railway</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The railway relays market comprises three primary types: Signalling Relays, Rolling Stock Relays, and Auxiliary Relays. Signalling Relays are essential for controlling and managing train movements, ensuring safety in operations. Rolling Stock Relays are used within train locomotives and cars to manage power and control functions. Auxiliary Relays serve various secondary functions such as alarm systems, control circuits, and interlocking applications. Together, these relays enhance safety, efficiency, and operational reliability in the railway industry.</p></p>
