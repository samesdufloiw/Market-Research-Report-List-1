<p><p>The Bitter Melon Extract market is divided into two primary extraction methods: cold-press extraction and solvent extraction. Cold-press extraction involves mechanically pressing the bitter melon to extract its juice and beneficial compounds without heat, preserving its nutritional value. In contrast, solvent extraction uses chemical solvents to dissolve and isolate the active ingredients, potentially enhancing yield but risking the integrity of the extract. Both methods cater to different consumer preferences for purity, potency, and application in health products.</p></p>
