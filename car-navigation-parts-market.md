<p><p>Car navigation systems can be categorized into three main types: DVD-based, data-based, and GPS-based systems. DVD-based navigation utilizes preloaded map data stored on DVDs, allowing offline access but often limited to the information available on the discs. Data-based systems, on the other hand, rely on real-time data feeds, offering updated maps and traffic information via internet connectivity. GPS-based navigation uses satellite signals to provide location tracking and route guidance, often integrating additional data for enhanced navigation capabilities.</p></p>
