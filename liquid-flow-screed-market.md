<p><ul><li>Pumpable Calcium Sulphate Screed</li><li>Pumpable Cement Based Screed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid flow screed market encompasses two primary types: pumpable calcium sulphate screed and pumpable cement-based screed. Pumpable calcium sulphate screed offers a smooth finish, rapid setting times, and excellent thermal conductivity, making it suitable for underfloor heating systems. In contrast, pumpable cement-based screed is known for its high durability and versatility, suitable for various applications including industrial and commercial floors. Both types are favored for their efficient application and ability to cover large areas quickly.</p></p>
