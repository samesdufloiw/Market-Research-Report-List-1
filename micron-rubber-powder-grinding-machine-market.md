<p><ul><li>Shear Type</li><li>Friction Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Micron Rubber Powder Grinding Machine market is divided into two main types: shear type and friction type. Shear type machines utilize cutting blades to break down rubber into fine particles through high-speed rotation, providing efficient size reduction with minimal heat generation. In contrast, friction type machines rely on the heat generated from the friction between rubber particles and grinding surfaces, promoting finer grinding but potentially resulting in higher temperatures. Both types cater to distinct processing needs in rubber recycling and powder production.</p></p>
