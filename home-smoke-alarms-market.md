<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Smoke Alarms</li><li>Ionization Smoke Alarms</li><li>Combination Smoke Alarms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home smoke alarms market includes three primary types: photoelectric smoke alarms, ionization smoke alarms, and combination smoke alarms. Photoelectric alarms utilize light beams to detect smoke, making them effective for smoldering fires. Ionization smoke alarms, on the other hand, sense changes in ionized air caused by flaming fires. Combination smoke alarms integrate both technologies, providing broader protection against different fire types. Each type caters to specific needs, influencing consumer choice and market dynamics in home safety.</p></p>
