<p><p>Soft Magnetic Composites (SMCs) are materials that combine magnetic properties with ease of shaping and processing, making them highly suitable for applications in electrical devices. The unique composition of SMCs allows for reducing eddy current losses, improving overall efficiency in power applications.</p><p>The Soft Magnetic Composites Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.8% during the forecast period. Additionally, the trend towards miniaturization and lightweight design in electronics is propelling the adoption of SMCs.
