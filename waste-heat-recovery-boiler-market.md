<p><p>The Waste Heat Recovery Boiler market consists of various types, primarily including Water Tube Boilers and Fire Tube Boilers, along with others. Water Tube Boilers circulate water in tubes heated by waste gases, offering higher efficiency and quicker response times. Fire Tube Boilers, on the other hand, contain hot gases in tubes surrounded by water, making them easier to maintain and cost-effective for smaller applications. Other types may include heat exchangers and specialty designs, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
