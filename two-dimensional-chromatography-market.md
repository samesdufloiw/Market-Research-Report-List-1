In contrast, 2D liquid chromatography employs two liquid phases to separate non-volatile and thermally labile compounds, offering greater flexibility for diverse applications in pharmaceuticals, environmental analysis, and food safety. Both methods improve analytical resolution and sensitivity.</p></p>
