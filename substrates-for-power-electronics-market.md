<p><p>The power electronics substrate market includes various types, each serving distinct applications. Direct Bonded Copper (DBC) substrates provide excellent thermal conductivity and electrical performance, making them ideal for high-power applications. Active Metal Brazed (AMB) substrates offer enhanced thermal stability and reliability through a brazing process, suitable for demanding environments. Insulated Metal Substrates (IMS) combine an insulating layer with metal for effective heat dissipation, commonly used in LED and automotive applications. Other substrates encompass emerging materials and technologies catering to specific needs in power electronics.</p></p>
