<p><p>Usage-Based Insurance (UBI) encompasses various types tailored to individual driving behaviors. Pay-As-You-Drive Insurance charges premiums based on distance traveled, incentivizing reduced mileage. Pay-How-You-Drive Insurance adjusts rates according to driving habits like speed and braking. Distance-Based Insurance is a straightforward model focusing purely on the miles driven, while Pay-As-You-Go Insurance offers flexibility, allowing payments based on actual usage or trips. Together, these models promote safe driving and efficient coverage, aligning costs with actual risk.</p></p>
