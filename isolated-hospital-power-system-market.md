<p><strong>The Isolated Hospital Power System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IT system</li><li>TN-S system</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Isolated Hospital Power Systems are essential for ensuring reliable electricity in healthcare facilities. The IT system (isolated terre) promotes safety by isolating the neutral point, protecting against ground faults, and allowing for uninterrupted operation during disturbances. Conversely, the TN-S system (terre-neutre séparé) features a grounded neutral point, ensuring effective fault current management and enhanced protection. These market types cater to specific safety standards and operational needs, providing tailored electrical solutions for hospital environments.</p></p>
