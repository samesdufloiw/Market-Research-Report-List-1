<p><p>Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) services in the aerospace market encompass a range of techniques to evaluate materials and structures without causing damage. Visual inspections assess surface conditions, while liquid penetrant testing reveals surface-breaking flaws. Acoustic emission testing detects stress waves from active defects. Leak testing ensures airtight integrity, and radiography uses X-rays for internal inspection. Ultrasonic testing employs high-frequency sound waves for thickness measurements. Magnetic particle testing identifies surface and near-surface defects, while eddy-current testing analyzes conductive materials for flaws. Other methods may include thermography and electrical testing.</p></p>
