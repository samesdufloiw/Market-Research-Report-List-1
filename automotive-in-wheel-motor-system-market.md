<p><p>The automotive in-wheel motor system market can be categorized into two main types: DC motors and AC motors. DC motors are popular due to their simplicity, ease of control, and high torque at low speeds, making them suitable for electric vehicle applications. In contrast, AC motors, particularly those using induction and permanent magnet technologies, offer higher efficiency, better power-to-weight ratios, and enhanced performance at varying speeds. Both motor types contribute to advancements in electric vehicle design and efficiency.</p></p>
