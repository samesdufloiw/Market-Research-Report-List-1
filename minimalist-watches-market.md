<p><p>Minimalist watches encompass three primary market types: quartz, mechanical, and automatic. Quartz watches use a battery for accurate timekeeping, prized for their low maintenance and affordability. Mechanical watches are powered by intricate gears and springs, appealing to enthusiasts who appreciate craftsmanship and tradition. Automatic watches, a subset of mechanical types, wind automatically through wrist movement, combining convenience with traditional watchmaking. Each type offers distinct features and appeals to different consumer preferences within the minimalist watch segment.</p></p>
