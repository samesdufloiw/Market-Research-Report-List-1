<p><ul><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Water-based</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive coatings, adhesives, and sealants market comprises three primary types: solvent-based, water-based, and powder coatings. Solvent-based types offer strong adhesion and durability but may emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Water-based options are eco-friendly, providing lower emissions while maintaining effective performance. Powder coatings, applied electrostatically and cured under heat, are highly durable and environmentally friendly, lacking solvents altogether. Each type plays a critical role in enhancing vehicle aesthetics, protection, and functionality while catering to various regulatory and performance requirements.</p></p>
