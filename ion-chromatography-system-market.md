<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ion Chromatography System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange Chromatography</li><li>Ion Exclusion Chromatography</li><li>Ion Pair Chromatography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ion chromatography system market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. Ion exchange chromatography, which separates ions based on their affinity to a stationary phase, ideal for analyzing inorganic anions and cations.</p><p>2. Ion exclusion chromatography, designed to separate ions while excluding non-ionic substances, commonly used for organic acid analysis.</p><p>3. Ion pair chromatography, which employs ion-pairing reagents to facilitate the separation of ionic compounds in non-aqueous solutions, enhancing the analysis of complex mixtures. Each type serves specific analytical needs across various industries.</p></p>
