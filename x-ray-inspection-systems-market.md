<p><p>X-ray inspection systems encompass various technologies, notably Digital Radiography (DR) and Computed Tomography (CT). DR utilizes digital detectors to capture images quickly, providing real-time feedback and enhanced image quality. It’s widely used in medical imaging and industrial applications due to its efficiency. In contrast, CT combines multiple X-ray images to create cross-sectional views of an object, offering detailed internal insights. Both types are essential for non-destructive testing, quality control, and diagnostic purposes across various sectors.</p></p>
