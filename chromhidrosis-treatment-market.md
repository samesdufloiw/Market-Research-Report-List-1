<p><p>The chromhidrosis treatment market can be categorized into three main types: apocrine chromhidrosis, eccrine chromhidrosis, and pseudochromhidrosis. Apocrine chromhidrosis involves the secretion of color-stained sweat from apocrine glands, commonly affecting body areas like armpits. Eccrine chromhidrosis, on the other hand, is characterized by colored sweat from eccrine glands, often linked to certain medications or conditions. Pseudochromhidrosis refers to discolored sweat resulting from external factors, such as dyes or bacteria. Each type presents unique treatment needs and market dynamics.</p></p>
