<p><p>The road traffic simulation software market can be categorized into micro-level and macro-level traffic simulation types. Micro-level traffic simulation focuses on individual vehicle movements and interactions, allowing for detailed analysis of intersections, traffic signals, and driver behavior. In contrast, macro-level traffic simulation examines broader traffic flow patterns and network performance across larger regions. This type is used for city-wide planning and management. Together, these simulations help optimize traffic systems, improve safety, and enhance urban mobility strategies.</p></p>
