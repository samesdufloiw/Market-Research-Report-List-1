<p><p>The Polyalkylene Glycol (PAG) oil market is categorized into two main types: water-insoluble and water-soluble PAG oils. Water-insoluble PAG oils are primarily used in applications requiring high lubricity and stability in extreme temperatures, such as in automotive and industrial machinery. In contrast, water-soluble PAG oils are favored in applications such as metalworking fluids and hydraulic fluids due to their ability to mix with water, offering enhanced cooling and lubricating properties. Each type serves distinct industrial needs based on solubility.</p></p>
