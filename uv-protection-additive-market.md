<p><p>The UV protection additive market comprises different types that enhance material durability against ultraviolet radiation. **UV absorbers** absorb harmful UV rays, converting them into less damaging heat. **UV stabilizers** prevent degradation of materials, maintaining color and mechanical properties over time. **Reflective agents** reflect UV radiation, minimizing its penetration into the substrate. **Other** additives may include specialized compounds combining various protective functions or enhancing specific applications, further expanding the range of options for industries needing UV protection.</p></p>
