Helical antennas offer higher gain and circular polarization, ideal for robust satellite communication. Choke-ring antennas, characterized by their multi-layer design, excel in precision applications by minimizing multipath interference and enhancing signal reception. Together, these antennas cater to diverse applications, from handheld devices to precise surveying and navigation systems.</p></p>
