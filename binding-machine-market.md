<p><p>The binding machine market consists of two primary types: manual binding machines and electric binding machines. Manual binding machines are operated by hand, offering portability and cost-effectiveness for small-scale binding tasks, making them ideal for personal or low-volume use. In contrast, electric binding machines are powered by electricity, enabling faster and more efficient binding for larger projects, such as those required in offices or print shops. The choice between them often depends on the volume and frequency of binding needs.</p></p>
