<p><p>The food grade phosphates market comprises three main types: orthophosphates, polyphosphates, and metaphosphates. Orthophosphates are single phosphate units often used as leavening agents and acidity regulators. Polyphosphates are chains of phosphate molecules that enhance moisture retention and texture in processed foods. Metaphosphates, with their unique structural characteristics, are used for their stabilizing properties in various food products. Together, these phosphates play essential roles in food preservation, quality enhancement, and nutritional value, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
