<p><p>The commercial-grade dehumidifier market is primarily divided into two types: portable and non-portable dehumidifiers. In contrast, non-portable dehumidifiers are typically larger, more robust units installed in fixed locations, providing continuous moisture management in environments such as warehouses or large commercial buildings. Both types serve to enhance air quality and protect assets from humidity damage.</p></p>
