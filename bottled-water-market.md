<p><p>The bottled water market is primarily categorized into two types: natural mineral water and man-made mineral water. Natural mineral water is sourced directly from mineral springs, retaining its unique mineral content and chemical properties, often considered more healthful and refreshing. In contrast, man-made mineral water is artificially produced, usually by adding minerals to purified water, providing a customizable product that caters to diverse taste preferences. Both types serve to meet the growing consumer demand for convenient, healthy hydration options.</p></p>
