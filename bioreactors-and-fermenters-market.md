<p><p>The bioreactors and fermenters market comprises single-use and multiple-use bioreactors, each serving distinct needs in biomanufacturing. Single-use bioreactors are designed for one-time use, enhancing flexibility, reducing contamination risks, and minimizing cleaning and sterilization costs. In contrast, multiple-use bioreactors can be sterilized and reused, making them more cost-effective for long-term production. Both types are essential in pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and food industries, facilitating cellular processes like fermentation and cell culture to produce various biologics and bio-based products.</p></p>
