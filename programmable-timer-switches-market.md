<p><ul><li>DIN Rail Mount</li><li>Panel Mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The programmable timer switch market includes two primary types based on installation methods: DIN Rail Mount and Panel Mount. DIN Rail Mount switches are designed for easy installation in electrical distribution boards, providing a compact and organized solution in commercial and industrial settings. In contrast, Panel Mount switches are integrated directly into control panels or enclosures, offering flexibility for various applications. Both types enable automated control of electrical devices, enhancing energy efficiency and convenience in managing lighting, HVAC systems, and other equipment.</p></p>
