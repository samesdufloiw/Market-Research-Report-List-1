<p><strong>The Vacant Gelatin Capsules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gastric Soluble</li><li>Enteric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacant gelatin capsules market is segmented into gastric soluble and enteric types. Gastric soluble capsules dissolve in the stomach, allowing for quick release of the encapsulated ingredients, making them suitable for drugs requiring immediate absorption. In contrast, enteric capsules are designed to withstand stomach acidity and dissolve in the more neutral pH of the intestine. This is ideal for sensitive compounds that could be degraded by gastric fluids or for targeted drug release in the digestive tract.</p></p>
