<p><p>The industrial electricity meters market is divided into two main types: single-phase and three-phase electricity meters. Single-phase electricity meters are typically used in residential or small commercial settings, measuring the electrical consumption of lower power loads. In contrast, three-phase electricity meters cater to industrial and larger commercial users, effectively measuring higher power loads and providing more accurate data for complex energy usage patterns. Both meter types are essential for monitoring, billing, and managing electricity consumption across various sectors.</p></p>
