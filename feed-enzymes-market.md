<p><p>The feed enzymes market encompasses various enzyme types that enhance animal nutrition and digestion. Pectinase breaks down pectin in plant cell walls, improving nutrient absorption. Xylanase acts on hemicellulose, enhancing feed digestibility in monogastric animals. Cellulase facilitates the breakdown of cellulose, supporting fiber digestion. Mannanase improves the digestion of polysaccharides in certain feeds, while glucanase aids in breaking down beta-glucans present in grains. Together, these enzymes promote nutrient efficiency, growth performance, and overall animal health in livestock production.</p></p>
