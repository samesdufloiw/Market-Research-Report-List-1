<p><strong>The Rainfall Monitoring Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Siphon Rain Gauge</li><li>Tipping Bucket Rain Gauge</li><li>Ultrasonic Rain Gauge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rainfall monitoring equipment market features various types, including siphon rain gauges, tipping bucket rain gauges, and ultrasonic rain gauges. Siphon rain gauges collect rainwater in a container, triggering a siphoning effect for measurement, while tipping bucket rain gauges utilize a funnel that directs rain into a bucket, which tips when it fills to a certain level, recording rainfall amounts. Ultrasonic rain gauges use sound waves to measure rainfall, providing real-time data without mechanical components, enhancing accuracy and durability.</p></p>
