<p><p>The wireless combustible gas detector market is divided into three main types based on connectivity technology: NB-IoT, 5G, and LoRa. NB-IoT (Narrowband IoT) offers reliable long-range connections with low power consumption, ideal for urban environments. 5G provides high-speed, low-latency communication, suitable for real-time monitoring and applications requiring instant data feedback. LoRa (Long Range) enables long-distance transmission with low power, making it effective for remote areas. Each technology meets specific needs in gas detection applications across varied settings.</p></p>
