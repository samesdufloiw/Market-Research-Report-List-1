<p><ul><li>CMOS Atomic Oscillators</li><li>Sine Atomic Oscillators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The atomic oscillators market comprises various types, notably CMOS atomic oscillators and sine atomic oscillators. CMOS atomic oscillators leverage complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, offering low power consumption and high integration capabilities, making them ideal for portable devices. In contrast, sine atomic oscillators provide a pure sine wave output, ensuring high stability and accuracy in frequency generation, often used in telecommunications and precision instruments. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing timing elements in modern electronic systems.</p></p>
