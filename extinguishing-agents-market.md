<p><p>The extinguishing agents market comprises various types of fire suppression substances tailored for specific applications. Halotron extinguishing agents are environmentally safer alternatives for halon, effective against flammable liquid fires. Foam extinguishing agents smother fires by creating a blanket over flammable liquids. Carbon dioxide extinguishing agents displace oxygen, suffocating fires without residue. Dry chemical extinguishing agents interrupt the chemical reaction of fire, while wet chemical extinguishing agents are designed for kitchen fires. Other agents include specialized powders and gases for niche applications.</p></p>
