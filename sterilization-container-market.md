<p><p>The sterilization container market features two primary types: valve type and filter type. Valve type containers utilize a mechanical valve to manage airflow and pressure during the sterilization process, ensuring effective steam penetration while preventing contamination. In contrast, filter type containers incorporate high-efficiency filters that allow for gas exchange while keeping contaminants at bay, supporting sterilization efficiency. Both types enhance the safety and integrity of medical instruments, making them essential in healthcare settings for maintaining sterile environments.</p></p>
