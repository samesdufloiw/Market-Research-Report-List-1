<p><p>The industrial centrifuge machines market encompasses various types designed for different separation processes. Sedimentation centrifuges utilize centrifugal force for solid-liquid separation, while filtering centrifuges aid in separating solids from liquids through a filter medium. Basket centrifuges are suited for batch processes, enhancing solid recovery. Scroll screen centrifuges efficiently separate particulates, while peeler centrifuges discharge separated solids automatically. Pusher centrifuges facilitate continuous solid removal for high-volume applications. Additionally, the "Others" category includes specialized centrifuges tailored for unique industrial needs.</p></p>
