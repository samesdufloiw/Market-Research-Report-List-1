<p><p>The wall-mounted rimless toilet market is divided into two main types: monolithic and separate. Monolithic toilets feature a single-piece design, offering a seamless look and easy cleaning, combining both the bowl and tank in one unit. In contrast, separate wall-mounted toilets consist of a bowl mounted on the wall with a concealed cistern behind it, allowing for more flexibility in design and installation. Both types prioritize hygiene, aesthetics, and space-saving in modern bathroom environments.</p></p>
