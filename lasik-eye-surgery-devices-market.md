<p><strong>The Lasik Eye Surgery Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Excimer Laser</li><li>Femtosecond Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LASIK eye surgery devices market comprises two primary types: excimer lasers and femtosecond lasers. Excimer lasers are used for reshaping the cornea by precisely removing tissue to correct vision errors like myopia, hyperopia, and astigmatism. Femtosecond lasers, on the other hand, create corneal flaps with high precision using rapid light pulses, enhancing the safety and effectiveness of the procedure. Both technologies are integral to the LASIK procedure, catering to diverse patient needs and advancing vision correction techniques.</p></p>
