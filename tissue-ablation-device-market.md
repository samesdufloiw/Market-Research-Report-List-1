<p><p>The tissue ablation device market comprises several types, each utilizing different technologies for targeted tissue destruction. Radiofrequency ablation employs electrical currents to generate heat, effectively removing or modifying tissue. Ultrasound ablation uses high-frequency sound waves for precision targeting. Laser-based ablation employs focused light to vaporize tissue. Cryoablation relies on extreme cold to freeze and destroy unwanted cells. Other methods may include microwave ablation or electroporation, catering to various medical applications and enhancing treatment efficiency across specialties.</p></p>
