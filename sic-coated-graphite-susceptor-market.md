<p><p>The SiC coated graphite susceptor market primarily includes two types: pancake susceptors and barrel susceptors. Pancake susceptors are flat, disc-shaped structures designed for even heating in processes such as semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, barrel susceptors are cylindrical and typically used in applications requiring a larger surface area for uniform thermal distribution. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing manufacturing efficiency by ensuring consistent heat delivery to substrates, thereby improving product quality and yield in high-temperature processes.</p></p>
