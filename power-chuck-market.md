<p><p>Power chuck market types are categorized based on the number of clamping jaws used in the design. Single claw chucks use one jaw for grip, suitable for lightweight applications. Double claw chucks offer greater stability for medium workpieces with two jaws. Three claw chucks provide improved centering and versatility, making them ideal for various shapes. Four claw chucks enable precise hold on asymmetrical workpieces, offering enhanced grip and stability, particularly in complex machining operations. Each type serves specific requirements in machining applications.</p></p>
