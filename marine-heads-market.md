<p><p>The marine heads market primarily comprises two types: cassette toilets and pump-out toilets. Cassette toilets are compact, portable units where waste is stored in a removable tank, making them easy to empty at designated facilities. Pump-out toilets, on the other hand, are plumbed systems that require a direct connection to a waste disposal system, utilizing a pump to remove waste. Both systems cater to the needs of boaters, ensuring sanitation onboard while complying with environmental regulations.</p></p>
