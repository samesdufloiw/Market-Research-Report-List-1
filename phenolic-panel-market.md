<p><p>The phenolic panel market primarily consists of two types: sandwich panels and ordinary panels. Sandwich panels feature a core layer sandwiched between two outer layers, enhancing thermal insulation, strength, and lightweight characteristics, ideal for construction and transportation applications. In contrast, ordinary panels are single-layered, offering basic insulation and durability, typically used for less demanding environments. Both panel types serve various sectors, including construction, automotive, and logistics, where moisture resistance, fire safety, and insulation are crucial.</p></p>
