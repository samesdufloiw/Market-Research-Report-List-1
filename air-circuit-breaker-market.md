<p><strong>The Air Circuit Breaker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Blast Circuit Breaker</li><li>Plain Air Circuit Breaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Air Circuit Breaker (ACB) market primarily includes two types: Air Blast Circuit Breaker and Plain Air Circuit Breaker. Air Blast Circuit Breakers utilize a high-pressure air blast to extinguish the electrical arc created during operation, making them suitable for high voltage applications. In contrast, Plain Air Circuit Breakers rely on ambient air for arc extinction, offering simpler design and lower cost for lower voltage applications. Both types are essential for protecting electrical systems from overcurrents and short circuits.</p></p>
