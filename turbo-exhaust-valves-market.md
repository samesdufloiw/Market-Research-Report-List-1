<p><strong>The Turbo Exhaust Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flange</li><li>Welded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The turbo exhaust valves market is divided into two main types: flange and welded. Flange-type exhaust valves utilize a bolted connection, allowing for easier installation and maintenance, making them a preferred choice in many applications. In contrast, welded turbo exhaust valves offer a more permanent attachment, providing enhanced durability and resistance to high-pressure environments. The choice between these types often depends on specific application requirements, such as ease of maintenance or the need for stronger, leak-proof connections.</p></p>
