<p><p>The spacecraft on-board computer market is categorized into three main segments based on orbital types. Low Earth Orbit (LEO) focuses on satellites that operate closer to Earth, benefiting from reduced latency and lower launch costs. Medium Earth Orbit (MEO) typically includes navigation and communication satellites offering global coverage. Geostationary Earth Orbit (GEO) involves satellites that maintain a fixed position relative to the Earth's surface, ideal for telecommunications and broadcasting. Each segment addresses specific mission needs and technological requirements for space operations.</p></p>
