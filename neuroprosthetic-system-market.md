<p><strong>The Neuroprosthetic System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Output Neural Prosthetics</li><li>Input Neural Prosthetics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The neuroprosthetic system market is categorized into output and input neural prosthetics. Output neural prosthetics, such as deep brain stimulators and motor neuron interfaces, restore or enhance motor functions by stimulating specific brain areas or peripheral nerves. Conversely, input neural prosthetics, including brain-computer interfaces and sensory prosthetics, focus on capturing neural signals to enable communication or restore sensory perception. Both types aim to improve the quality of life for individuals with neurological impairments, offering innovative solutions for rehabilitation and assistance.</p></p>
