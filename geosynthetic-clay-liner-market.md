<p><p>The Geosynthetic Clay Liner (GCL) market features various types based on performance metrics. The Bentonite Swell Index measures the swelling capacity of bentonite; Fluid Loss assesses the amount of water loss during hydration. Mass/Area indicates the density of bentonite in GCLs. Grab Strength evaluates the tensile strength of GCLs, while Peel Strength measures adhesive performance. Index Flux assesses permeability, Hydraulic Conductivity gauges water flow resistance, and Hydrated Internal Shear Strength evaluates stability under saturated conditions. These parameters ensure reliable performance in environmental applications.</p></p>
