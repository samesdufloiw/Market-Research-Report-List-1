<p><p>Concentrated Photovoltaic (CPV) systems harness solar energy by using lenses or mirrors to focus sunlight onto high-efficiency solar cells. The market is divided into High Concentrated Photovoltaic (HCPV) systems, which utilize a high concentration ratio (typically above 200 suns) and require precise tracking mechanisms. In contrast, Low Concentrated Photovoltaic (LCPV) systems operate with lower concentration ratios (usually below 200 suns), making them simpler and less costly to deploy. Both types aim to enhance energy efficiency and reduce land footprint.</p></p>
