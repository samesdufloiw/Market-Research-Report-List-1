<p><p>The automatic hermetic door market is primarily categorized into two types: single leaf and double leaf doors. Single leaf doors consist of one panel that opens in a single direction, ideal for smaller spaces or where a compact design is needed. Double leaf doors, featuring two panels that swing open, are suited for wider openings, facilitating higher traffic flow and accessibility in hospitals and clean rooms. Both types enhance hygiene and energy efficiency in sterile environments.</p></p>
