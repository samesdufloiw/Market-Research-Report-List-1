<p><ul><li>PSA Nitrogen Generators</li><li>Membrane Nitrogen Generators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Food and Beverage Nitrogen Generators market primarily features two types: PSA (Pressure Swing Adsorption) Nitrogen Generators and Membrane Nitrogen Generators. PSA generators utilize a two-chamber system that alternates between adsorption and regeneration phases to purify nitrogen from compressed air, providing high purity levels. Conversely, membrane nitrogen generators utilize selective permeation through polymer membranes to separate nitrogen from oxygen, offering a more compact and simpler solution with lower operational costs. Both serve crucial roles in food preservation and packaging processes.</p></p>
